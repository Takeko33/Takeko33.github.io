请假的表达方式

  1. I ‘d like to have three days off.
  2. Would it be possible if I take next Friday off?
  3. I have some personal business that I need to take care of.
  4. Could you cover for me on Friday?
  5. Would it be possible if I swiched my Tuesday shift for your Friday shift?

verb + sb + prep + the + 身体部位

eg：take sb by the arm 抓住某人的胳膊

pat sb on the shoulder 拍某人的肩膀

verb: hit/strike/beat/touch/take/catch

the: 只用the，不用a/an或his/her

prep: in/on/by/with/at

1. in

表示在身体部位内部、涉及身体表面较柔软或较“空”的部位也用in；以及表示身体部位受到伤害或者感到疼痛时，也常用in

He hit me in the mouth(eye/stomach/face)

I was wounded in the leg.

He felt a sharp pain in his knee.

2. on

表示身体部位表面和面部表情，或者较为坚硬、突出的部分。

He stared at her with tahet peculiar expressions on his face.

He was hit on the head .

The man beat the boy on the back/breast/jaw/neck.

The mother kissed her daughter on both cheeks.

3. at

在身体部位名词前用介词at的时候不多，一般出现在下面这些用法中（at后面不用冠词）

at heart：Yeah，but I felt sick at heart when I saw how much she was hurt.

at hand: He said the road for oppasition has been long and painful, but that victory is at hand.

4. by

常与动词take，seize，catch，lead等词连用，表示拉、扯、抓住身体某部位

He took her by the hand.

The man caught/seized the thief by the arm

You should make your own decisions, and not let others lead you by the nose.

5. with

常表示借助什么方法、工具等做某事

take sth with both hands 用双手拿

see with one’s eyes 用眼看
You can’t imagine how amazing they are unless you see them with your own eyes.

颜色的意义

Blue

  1. 表示情绪–不快乐、忧郁

    This is because blue was related to rain, or storms, and in Greek mythology, the god Zeus would make rain when he was sad (crying) , and a storm when he was angry.–Wikipedia

    in a blue mood 心情郁闷

    a blue outlook 悲观的人生观

  2. 下流

    blue talk 下流的言论

    blue film 成人电影

    a blue joke 下流的笑话

  3. 表示社会地位高、出身贵族

    “蓝血贵族”源于西班牙皇室，贵族们常自豪的挽起袖管展示自己雪白小臂上清晰可见的蓝色静脉血管。

    How I wish I were a blue blood, so that I could be a little princess quietly.

  4. blue in the face 精疲力尽
  5. blue fear 极度恐慌
  6. once in a blue moon 几乎不，千载难逢
  7. drink till all’s blue 一醉方休

Green

  1. green可指代财力的，由于美元背面是绿色的。

    green power（财团）

    green back（美钞，又称“绿背”）

  2. 用于描述嫉妒

源于William Shakespeare的《奥赛罗》—— O, beware, my lord, of jealousy, it is the green-eyed monster which doth mock the meat it feeds on.

  1. green是大多数正在生长的植物的颜色，意味着年轻，因此green也有生疏的意思。

    green as grass 幼稚的

    in the green 在青春期

    green from school 初入社会

    greenhorn 新手——
    horn是动物头上长的角，动物在刚出生的时候，它们的角还没有长成，只是头上有日后会长成角的基部，带有不久便会消退的绿色，所以greenhorn是初生动物的标志，现在引申为新手的意思。

Yellow

在古代中国黄色一般象征着皇权，不过西方国家yellow比较含贬义，据《圣经》（Bible）记载，犹大是耶稣的门徒之一，也是出卖耶稣的人，他就身穿黄色的衣服，因此后人认为黄色有卑鄙、胆小等象征意义。

yellow dog 杂种

yellow belly 胆小鬼

Red

red在西方文化中是一个贬义较强的词，因为西方人较多地将红色与血液的颜色联系在一起，由鲜血再联系到流血的暴力，所以Red除了红色的本意外还有暴力、危险的意思。

red rules 暴力统治

red battle 血战

red light 危险信号

red alert 警报
