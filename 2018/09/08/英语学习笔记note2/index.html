English

The Pursuit of Happyness

“芝士派”无字幕电影220180902-20180908学习笔记

take sb/sth for granted

认为…..理所当然

We take having an endless supply of water for granted.

jerk around

耍弄，戏弄

You jerk me around on this, and I’m going to get cranky right in your face.

blow away

留下深刻印象

The amount of the check blew me away.

point blank

直截了当地

I asked a favor of him once, but he refuse point-blank.

break it up

口语中表示“散了吧”

Alright, show’s over. Break it up, break it up.

break the ice

打破僵局
The host told a joke to his guests to break the ice.

slice in line/cut in line

插队
I get annoyed when people slice in line.

That’s it.

  1. 表示赞同 =That’s right

    – So she is going to marry the doctor.

    – That’s it.

  2. 表示结束，意为“完了，没有别的了”

    I’ll finish taht, that’s it.

  3. 表示反感或失望，意为“够了，行了，得了”

    That’s it. Haven’t you said enough？

  4. 表示鼓励或赞同，意为“这就对了，就这样”

    That’s it. Let’s tell her the news when we see her.

用于称赞的话

How smart of you! 你真明智

You should have a real talent. 你真有天赋

That’s a very nice hairdo you’re wearing. 你发型真漂亮

You are looking great. 你真好看

That’s the icing on the cake. 这可真是双喜临门了

A good teacher makes good student. 名师出高徒

I would like to compliment you on your diligence. 我想称赞一下你的勤奋

We’re proud of you. 我们为你自豪

He has a long head. 他很有远见卓识
