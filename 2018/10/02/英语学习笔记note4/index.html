Confessions of a Shopaholic

“芝士派”无字幕电影20180923-20180930学习笔记

rip up

撕碎

He ripped the letters up and threw them in the wastepaper basket.

rip off

敲竹杠，坑钱
The company has been accused of ripping off customers.

in the middle of something

正在忙于做某事

He is in the middle ofvshopping.

swap meet

跳蚤市场

Tom takes his old sofa to the swap meet and see if he can exchange it for a television.

far out

异乎寻常的

Fantasies cannot harm you, no matter how bizarre or far out they are.

thrive on

因…蓬勃发展

More and more people use social networks at work, which thrive on exchanging information.

check it out

过来看，口语化的表达，一般用于让身边的人来看看这个东西

Check it out. she’s staring at you.

up to one’s eyeball in

忙的焦头烂额

I’m up to my eyeball in work.

part + 国籍

表示有部分那个国家的血统或者语言文化能力

My mother is Chinese and my father is a US resident, so I’m part Chinese.

I want you to meet Janne from Nokia. I told him you’re part Finnish, and he’s very excited to meet you.

down to sb

由…引起，由…造成 = owe to

The mistake is enirely dowm to us.
